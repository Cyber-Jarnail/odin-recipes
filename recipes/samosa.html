Samosa

A favorite Punjabi delicacy loved by all ages.

Great for parties, birthdays or any event...even as a snack.

Enjoy the golden crispy treat loved by many. Stuffed with potatoes and peppers.

Its aroma and taste will have you keep coming back for more.

Ingredients

    Flour
    Oil
    Potatoes
    Peppers
    The love for good food!

Steps

Begin by mixing oil and fluor followed by kneeding the dough.

Let the dough sit for about 30 minutes and in the mean time prepare the stuffing.

Stuffing includes potatoes and peppers. Boil the potatoes and chop the peppers.

Once the potatoes are done, mash them.

Now mix the potatoes and peppers together.

Now take the dough and divide it (how many will depend on how many you want to make).

Take each piece of dough and flatten it into a circle.

Take flattened dough and cut it in half. Now shape each half of the dough into a cone.

Repeat with other pieces of dough.

Take the mix of potatoes and peppers and stuff them into the dough. Don't overfill.

Close the open end of the dough by squeezing the edges together.

Heat oil into a fry pan. Drop a piece of dough to see if the oil is ready. The dough should float back up once cooked.

Insert the samosas into the oil, few at a time. Cook until they are a golden brown.

Once finished they can be eaten by themselves, ketchup or chutney.
