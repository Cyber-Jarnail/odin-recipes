Whats for breakfast, lunch and dinner? Kheer

Popular delicacy that can be enjoyed any time of the day.

Eat it to your hearts content.

Excellent for the hot summer days or winter evenings.

Ingredients

    1/4 cup of Basmati Rice
    1 liter of Whole Milk
    1/4 cup of Water
    1/3 to 4 tablespoons of Sugar
    Optional ingredients include sweet raisins or chopped nuts

Steps

Wash the basmati rice and soak in water for about 30 minutes.

If adding nuts, you can soak them into hot water, peel them and chop them.

Add water into the pot followed by the milk. Water will keep the kheer from burning at the bottom.

Boil at medium heat.

When milk begins to boil, add the rice. Keep stirring the pot to prevent the rice from burning.

The rice should slowly being to become mushy. The starch from the rice will thicken the kheer.

Add the amount of sugar you would like.

You may add sweet raisins or nuts if you prefer.

Keep stirring and cook the kheer at low heat until it thickens but flowing consistency.

Some prefer to eat it hot, if not, let it cool before eating.

Enjoy!
