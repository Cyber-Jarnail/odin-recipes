"Jalebi Baby"

The crispy golden sweet loved by all ages.

Perfect for any occasion.

Make sure you make enough, cause guests will come for seconds.

Ingredients

    1/2 cup Ghee
    3 cups of all purpose flour
    2 cups of hung curd
    3 cups of water
    3 cups of sugar
    1/2 teaspoon of powdered green cardamom
    1 1/2 pinch of baking soda
    5 strands of saffron
    1/2 cup of corn flour
    2 cups of sunflower oil
    4 drops of rose essence
    1/2 teaspoon of food coloring

Steps

Begin by mixing the all purpose flour, baking soda and corn flour into a bowl.

Add ghee and food coloring and continue to mix.

Add hung curd and water to make the batter thick. (Make sure the consistency still has a flow)

Keep the mix to the side for about 8 to 10 hours to allow it to ferment.

Make the sugar syrup by boiling water and mixing sugar into it until it fully dissolves.

Once the syrup attains one string consistency, add cardamom power, rose essence and saffron.

Mix the syrup well.

Take the jalebi batter and fill a large piece of cloth or a bottle with a small hole.

Squeeze the batter into pan filled with oil, at medium heat.

Fry the jalebi's until they are a nice golden color. Remove from pan.

Warm the syrup and pour it over the jalebi's.

Enjoy!
